Origin of GSS Aleyita
Government Secondary School, Aleyita is a mixed secondary school that was established in the year 2025. The school was founded to provide quality education to both boys and girls, helping
them develop academically and socially in a supportive learning environment.

The school is located in Aleyita along Airport Road, Abuja. This location makes the school
easily accessible and places it in a peaceful area that is suitable for teaching and learning activities.

Government Secondary School, Aleyita places great importance on academic excellence.
Teachers are dedicated to delivering quality instruction that helps students gain knowledge, skills, and confidence needed for further education and future careers.

In addition to academics, the school emphasizes good morals and the fear of God. Students
are taught values such as honesty, discipline, respect, and responsibility, which help shape their character and behavior both in and outside the school.

The school is driven by strong values that guide all its actions and decisions. These values
support the school’s vision of raising well-rounded students who are academically sound, morally upright, and prepared to contribute positively to society.

Our MISSIONS
Our mission is to be the leading Government Secondary School in FCT and Nigeria. We aim to achieve academic excellence, producing students
who excel in their studies and beyond. Our goal is to create a learning environment that fosters growth, curiosity, and success.

We strive to be admired for our students' outstanding performance, both in academics and extracurricular activities. We believe in nurturing
well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to society.

Our staff are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and dedication. We aim to create a culture of trust, respect,
and collaboration among students, teachers, and the community.

We are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment that allows every student to reach their full potential. We believe in empowering students
with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in life.

By achieving our mission, we aim to become a model school, inspiring others to strive for excellence. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on our
students, community, and Nigeria as a whole.

Our AIMS
1. Develop a whole school behavior policy supported by all community members.

2. Encourage good behavior through rewards for students of all ages and abilities.

3. Clearly define minor and serious misconduct and corresponding sanctions.

4. Treat problems with firmness, fairness, and a focus on improving behavior.

5. Foster a positive school culture that promotes respect and responsibility.

6. Provide opportunities for students to develop self-discipline and self-regulation.

7. Support students in understanding the consequences of their actions.

8. Encourage staff to model good behavior and reinforce positive student behavior.

9. Develop a consistent approach to behavior management across the school.

10. Involve parents and the community in promoting good behavior.
